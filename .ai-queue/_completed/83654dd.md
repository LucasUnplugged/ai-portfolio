2. **Stat cards row:** 4 `StatCard` components in a responsive grid:

   - Documents (total count from documents data, icon: FileText)
   - Open Tasks (count tasks not "done", icon: CheckSquare)

3. **Projects section:** Header "Projects" with a link-style "View all" text. Grid of `ProjectCard` components (show all projects). Responsive: 3 columns on desktop, 2 on tablet, 1 on mobile.
